  • csv

    Return tensor representation in unformatted, comma separated list.

    Elements of a row are comma separated. Rows are separated by newlines. Higher dimensional slices are separated by a line consisting entirely of semicolons, where the number of semicolons indicates the dimension that ended; e.g. ; comes between matrices in a 3D tensor, ;; comes between 3D tensors in a 4D tensor, ;;; between 4D tensors in 5D, etc.
